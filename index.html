About Me

I am an adventurous and curious student at STI Balagtas working toward a bachelor’s degree, fascinated by technology, new roles, and “impossible” problems. I thrive in changing environments where I can take risks, learn fast, and adapt my skills to whatever’s needed.

I’ve held leadership roles in student organizations—leading teams, managing projects, and leveraging teammates’ strengths to deliver meaningful results.
